Title

The page title, often referred to as the ‘title tag’, ‘meta title’ or sometimes ‘SEO title’ is an HTML element in the head of a webpage that should succinctly describe the content and purpose of the page to users and search engines. Page titles are used by search engines more than 80% of the time for the titles used in their search results
